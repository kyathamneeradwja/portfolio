2.POLICE STATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Police Station Management System (PSMS) is a desktop application that aims at computerizing and simplifying the daily activities of a police station. It offers an effective platform to file, track, and maintain First Information Reports (FIRs) and other case histories. Major features are: FIR Filing Module: Crime or civil cases details can be input by citizens or authorized personnel using simple forms. Database Integration: Every record is safely stored in a MongoDB database for rapid retrieval and updating. Administrative Dashboard: An all-screen Tkinter-based dashboard enables police officials to display, search, and handle cases easily. Document Management: Facilitates uploading files (evidence, reports, and case files) for storing in a centralized repository. Role-based Access: Protects data privacy by granting access or editing of confidential records only to privileged users (officers or administrators). By substituting a manual register with an automated system, this project aids in enhancing data accuracy, response time, and overall efficiency in police station operations. It can further be extended with features such as crime analytics, SMS/email alerts, and integration with other law-enforcement databases.
